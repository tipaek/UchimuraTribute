KOHEI UCHIMURA

The Greatest Male Gymnast of All Time

Kohei Uchimura has had the most dominant career out of any gymnast ever, winning every major title in a single Olympic cycle.

Undeniably a Star

With 7 Olympic Medals, 21-time World Medalist, and 6 Consecutive World All-Round Titles, Uchimura is largely considered to be the greatest male gymnast of all time.
